Lee Si-young is a South Korean actress, model, and former boxer.

She was born on April 17, 1982, in Seoul, South Korea. Lee Si-young gained popularity for her acting skills in both television dramas and films.

Lee Si-young also appeared in the survival variety show "Korea's Next Top Model" and has worked as a model in the fashion industry. She is also a well-known figure for her charitable work, particularly in the area of education.

A bus park is a designated area or facility where buses are parked.

Bus parks are commonly found in cities, towns, and transportation hubs and are integral to public transport systems.
